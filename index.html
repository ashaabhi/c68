Aman Setu My School

Conceptualised, financed and set up by Madhavi Kapur Foundation, Aman Setu is a beautiful school in Pune. Set in Wagholi, it is within easy reach by car and bus. At Aman Setu, we nurture every child ensuring that he/she achieves his/her inner potential.
